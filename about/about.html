I’m Shreyas, a graphic designer working behind Pixel Design Studio. I enjoy creating clean, modern visuals that feel purposeful and easy to connect with. Most of my work revolves around branding, social media creatives, advertisements (OOH) and digital design.

For me, good design goes beyond how it looks. It’s about communicating the right message, keeping things clear, through graphic design as a visual artform. I’m constantly learning, experimenting, and refining my skills to create work that feels fresh and relevant.
