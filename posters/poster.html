Pixel Design work about Studio.
VISUAL LAB 01

A collection of poster explorations where ideas, concepts, and visual experiments take shape. Each piece serves as a playground for translating thoughts and inspirations into bold visual forms. allowing concepts and creative thoughts to evolve into expressive graphic.

Past Lives

This poster interprets Past Lives through a cinematic editorial layout built on layered textures and structured grids. Character driven imagery and restrained typography create a calm visual rhythm that reflects themes of memory, distance, and connection, translating the film’s quiet emotional depth into a balanced and atmospheric visual composition

Psychosis

Donnie’s Psychosis translates the unsettling inner world of Donnie Darko into a fragmented visual narrative, symbolizing distorted perception and temporal confusion. The collage reflects a mind caught between reality and hallucination, echoing the film’s haunting exploration of fate, time loops, and psychological isolation.

Donda

Expanding from the stark black silence of Donda’s original cover, this poster visualizes the album’s emotional and spiritual landscape. Monochrome textures and performance imagery merge to reflect themes of faith, loss, and redemption. The composition balances stillness and intensity, translating the album’s complex atmosphere into a dramatic visual narrative.

Eclipse

Inspired by the Eclipse arc of Berserk, this poster transforms the story’s brutality and tragedy into a layered visual narrative. Fragments of characters and symbolic imagery converge around the imposing God Hand, representing the moment fate reshapes the world of the series. The stark dual tone palette echoes Miura’s haunting ink style and the arc’s overwhelming sense of despair.
