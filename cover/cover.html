COVER VAULT 01

A collection of redesigned album covers that reinterpret existing artworks through a new visual lens. Each piece explores an alternative design direction while preserving the original essence, mood, and identity of the record, offering a fresh visual take on familiar releases.

Sin Decade

Inspired by the theatrical decadence of the original Sin Decade by Pretty Maids, this redesign transforms the crowded historical scene into a bold symbolic composition. The restrained layout modernizes the album identity while preserving its dark heavy-metal atmosphere.
